## 🧩 Overview
**College Escape** is my first 3D Unity project focused on exploration, puzzle-solving, and stealth gameplay.

The player navigates through a realistic college campus, completing tasks, collecting items, and unlocking areas while avoiding an enemy. The game combines **strategy, exploration, and survival elements** to create an engaging experience.

---

## 🎯 Gameplay
- 🏫 Explore a detailed 3D campus  
- 🎯 Complete objectives to progress  
- 🔑 Collect keys, clues, and perks  
- 🔐 Unlock restricted areas  
- 👹 Avoid a roaming enemy AI  
- 🕵️ Use stealth mechanics (hiding, timing, smart movement)  

---

## 👹 Enemy System
A roaming enemy adds challenge and tension:
- Patrol-based AI behavior  
- Detects player if too close  
- Forces strategic movement and hiding
